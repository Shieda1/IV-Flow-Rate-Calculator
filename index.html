What is the IV flow rate calculator?
The IV flow rate calculator is a tool that computes the correct drip rate of an intravenous fluid.
How to calculate IV flow rate?
To calculate IV flow rate you should use the following formula:
IV flow rate = drop factor * total volume / infusion time
Why use IV flow rate calculator?
The IV flow rate calculator is used in order to measure the correct drip rate of an intravenous fluid.
